About

Windermont is a platform for clear, independent analysis of public companies worldwide. We focus on mid-cap industrials—firms between $2B and $10B—offering short theses for retail investors and deep dives for hedge funds. Our goal: uncover opportunities others miss. Started in 2025, we're building a team to scale this vision. Stay tuned.

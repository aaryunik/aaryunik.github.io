About Me

I'm Nikhil, a sophomore studying Computer Science in the College of Engineering at Cornell University (Ithaca, NY).

I am interested in how math, computing, and physics can be applied in the real world. I have previously worked on algorithms, particle physics, and quantum computing projects!

I am an active volunteer and TA for camps, courses, and workshops conducted by NGOs such as Raising a Mathematician Foundation.

I love reading fiction novels, historical journals, eccentric poems, and short stories. I also enjoy creative writing. Some of my prose and poems are linked on the writing page.

I like listening to various musical genres, from melancholic folk to indie rock and everything in between (here are some of my Spotify playlists).

I occasionally try my hand at photography!
